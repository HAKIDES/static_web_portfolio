My Journey


My career began in the fast-paced world of retail, where I sharpened my skills in customer service and team supervision. From there, I transitioned into AutoCAD drafting, specializing in FDAS plans with laser-sharp precision and attention to detail.


But that was just the beginning.


At Brokenshire Community Health & Development Center, I expanded my toolkit—fusing hands-on IT support, Audio Visual technical expertise, and creative graphic design to help people connect through technology and visual storytelling. Whether I was troubleshooting systems or crafting impactful visuals, I focused on making technology an accessible and empowering tool, ensuring the successful operation of projects throughout my contract service.


Today, I’m diving deeper into my passion for programming and web development, eager to build smarter solutions and lead innovations that matter. I thrive at the intersection of logic and creativity, where ideas become experiences and systems serve as a powerful tool into making a much more easier working environment.
